Our team at ADA University started a fascinating web development project where we used HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create a dynamic website. We started our adventure with extensive instruction on Codecademy, where we improved our coding abilities and acquired a firm grasp of these crucial web technologies. There are four well-designed pages on our website, each with a certain function. Our team name is prominently displayed on the front page, which establishes the tone for a fun online experience. By visiting the Members page, users may explore comprehensive details on every member of the team, promoting openness and communication. A highlight is the Hour of Code project website, which includes an extensive report on our coding activities together with eye-catching graphics. This part not only demonstrates our technical proficiency but also offers a glimpse into our creative and cooperative problem-solving methodology. The last page explores the complexities of the entire undertaking and provides a thoughtful summary of how the website was developed. We provide visitors a behind-the-scenes peek at the development of our ADA University online project by sharing insights into the difficulties encountered and the victories attained during the project's lifecycle.
